SUBSCRIPTION RATES FOR 2023-2024

Rates are as follows.

Single £105

Family £210 (includes two adults and all children and full-time students living at the same address)

Students £50

Croquet only £60

Juniors aged 12-18 £30, aged under 12 £20 (under 12s must be accompanied by a responsible adult)

Non-playing £10

Visitor fees Adult £6 Children £2 Students £3. Each visitor may play up to three times in a season.

Returnable key deposit £18

If you would like to join, please contact Deirdre Pope at deirdre.pope@some.oxon.org. You are welcome to come and play as a visitor before deciding whether to join.

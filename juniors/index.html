JUNIORS

Juniors (under 18) are welcome to join AVTCC. They can join as part of a family membership (£190) or in their own right for £20 (under 12) or for £30 (12 and over). Under 12s must always be accompanied by a responsible adult while at the club.

There is a thriving coaching program for juniors who have enjoyed much success competing in the local tournaments.

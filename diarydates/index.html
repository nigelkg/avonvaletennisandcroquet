Club Suppers

These are relaxed, informal, social get-togethers for players and their guests which consist of simple two-course suppers in the Clubhouse, priced at £10 for members and £12 for non-members, on ad hoc Wednesdays  from  7:30  after the club tennis/croquet session.  Bring your own drinks!
